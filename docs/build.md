# 🛠️ Build Process

## Enclosure

- Wood panels cut and assembled with wood glue and screws
- Black painted front baffle, wood veneer on sides
- Internal bracing added to reduce panel vibration
- 3 cutouts per speaker: subwoofer (bottom), mid-woofer (centre), tweeter (top)

## Electronics Assembly

- Amp modules mounted to internal panels
- Passive crossover PCB positioned centrally
- DC-DC converter for regulated lower-voltage stages
- Wiring colour coded: yellow (signal), red (V+), black (GND)
- Hot-glue strain relief on all connections
- All electronics routed and secured through woofer bay during assembly

## Back Panel

- 2-pin AC socket for mains input
- Illuminated rocker power switch
- 4-terminal speaker binding posts
- 3× potentiometers on custom wood bracket for per-driver level trim

## Tuning

- Initial listening session to identify problem frequencies
- Peace EQ applied iteratively — adjust, listen, repeat
- Final preset saved as `eq/tight-bass.peace`
